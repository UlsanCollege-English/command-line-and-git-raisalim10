MY HOMETOWN🌿

Nestled in the lush eastern foothills of Nepal, Ilam is a picturesque paradise renowned for its breathtaking tea gardens, rolling hills, and serene landscapes🌿. This enchanting town, often referred to as the "Queen of Hills," captivates visitors with its serene ambiance and the aromatic allure of its world-famous tea. Ilam's tranquil beauty and the warm hospitality of its people make it a must-visit destination, offering a serene escape from the hustle and bustle of modern life.
